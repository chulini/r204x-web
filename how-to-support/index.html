How to support Resistance 204X?

Are you a GAMER?

1.- Wishlist the game on steam. It helps a lot with visibility and future success of the game.
Go to
https://store.steampowered.com/app/1829140/Resistance_204X/

and press “Add To Your Wishlist”.

2.- If you want to keep updated just follow the game in your favorite platform:

  • Twitter
  • Reddit
  • Gamejolt

3.- Join the Resistance on Discord and be part of an awesome community

Are you a TWITCH STREAMER or YOUTUBER?

Check out the game on Twitch or make a YouTube video about Resistance 204X. If you don’t have a steam code just send me a message asking for one. You can reach me on discord, email or twitter.

Here you have the media kit with transparent logos, launch trailer, and other media I prepared for you.

Make fan art of the game! I would love to put your fan art inside the game itself so I’m happy to receive your art on discord, email or twitter.
