Descrption

Designed to introduce new players into fighting games, Resistance 204X, is a fast-paced footsies-based 2D indie fighting game currently in development for PC and Mobile.

Fight against other players locally or cross-play online.

The goal is simple: KO your opponent, run fast as possible and cross the goal at the end of the stage to win the match.

Play strategically. Break your opponent’s guard with your combos, punish them at the right moment, or trick them with throws, pokes, and cross-ups.

The characters are controlled only with directions and two attack buttons, making the game very easy to learn. However, the fast pacing pushes the player to train his instant reaction, prediction, and deception, while at the same time, the platforming and the win condition encourage strategic positioning, resulting in deep and interesting gameplay.

Rollback Netcode ensures a responsive and low latency experience.
