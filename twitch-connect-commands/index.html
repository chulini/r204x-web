Twitch connect commands documentation

Feel free to share this documentation with anyone! The available Twitch commands for Resistance 204X are:

!invisible

Invisible Fighters
Fighters are not rendered. Streamer can only see the visual effects and the background.

!triple

Triple Air Jumps
Fighters are allowed to jump 3 time in the air.

!dinorex

Dinorex
Fighters becomes a dinosaur and everythong goes crazy.

!hitbox

Hitbox Only
Only fighter’s hitboxes are rendered.

!potato

Potato Quality
Game is rendered in the worst possible quality settings.

!shambles

Shambles
Both fighters swp places at random times.
