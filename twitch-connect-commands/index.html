Twitch commands

Feel free to share this documentation with anyone! The available twitch chat rules for Resistance 204X are:

Invisible Fighters

Command: !invisible

Fighters are not rendered. Streamer can only see the visual effects and the background.

Triple Air Jumps

Command: !triple
Fighters are allowed to jump/air-dash 3 times in the air.

Dinorex

Command: !dinorex
Everything goes wild. Literally.

Hitbox Only

Command: !hitbox
Only fighter’s hitboxes are rendered.

Potato Quality

Command: !potato
Game is rendered in the worst possible quality settings.

Shambles

Command: !shambles
Both fighters swap places at random times.
